# Issues

Backlog and known limitations for the **Radzen Blazor MCP**, captured from the
research reports (`docs/superpowers/research/`) and the multi-agent review pass.
Each note carries Obsidian-compatible YAML frontmatter and `[[wikilinks]]`; open
the repo as an Obsidian vault to browse them with graph view and backlinks.

Frontmatter convention: `status` (open · deferred · accepted), `category`
(feature · enhancement · limitation), `area`, `priority`, `created`, `tags`.
